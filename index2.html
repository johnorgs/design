Pictures

In photography there is a reality so subtle that it becomes more real than reality. Wherever there is light, one can photograph. The best images are the ones that retain their strength and impact over the years, regardless of the number of times they are viewed. The camera makes you forget you’re there. It’s not like you are hiding but you forget, you are just looking so much. You don’t take a photograph, you make it. There are always two people in every picture: the photographer and the viewer. In photography there are no shadows that cannot be illuminated. Photography helps people to see. Photographing a culture in the here and now often means photographing the intersection of the present with the past. A photograph is a secret about a secret. The more it tells you the less you know.
